How to Use

To begin using U-Plant, first log in. Notice the instructions in the middle of the home page, these will help guide you through the main functionality of the application.

FAQ

Forget your password?
- You can reset it on the settings page or on the login page

Cannot find your plant?
- Navigate to the plant catalog page and type in your plant into the search bar

Problems planting in your garden?
- Delete and replace the problematic plants

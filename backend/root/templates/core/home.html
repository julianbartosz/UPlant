To Plant a Garden:

  • Navigate to the Garden Dashboard
  • Create a garden if you haven't already
  • Select your planting plots
  • Search for your desired plant on the left
  • Drag and drop it onto your garden

To Search for a Plant:

  • Navigate to the Plant Catalog
  • Use the search bar on the left
  • Click on a search result
  • Plant info appears on the right

Notifications:

  • Navigate to the Plant Dashboard
  • On the right hand side you will see a box with a grid inside, these are your notifications
  • Click the green plus to create a notification, a red X to delete it
  • Click the green plus then select the plants the notification applies to, its name, and the time interval in days you would like to be notified
  • After doing so, you may view your notifications and their color code by toggling the switch above box
